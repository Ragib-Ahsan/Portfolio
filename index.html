Philosophy

I specialize in breaking down ambiguous problems into clear product requirements, workflows, and measurable outcomes by bridging the gap between business, users, and engineering. The approach is rooted in systems thinking, stakeholder alignment, and practical execution.

My interest particularly lies in how products behave in the real world especially in environments where infrastructure, user habits, and operational realities are imperfect. Rather than designing for ideal scenarios, the focus remains on simplifying complexity and building solutions that people can realistically adopt, use consistently, and derive long-term value from.
